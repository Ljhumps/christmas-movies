What makes a Christmas movie?

'What makes a Christmas movie?'' is often debated (Die Hard is a Christmas movie, get over it!). So, allow me to define what a Christmas movie is: A Christmas movie is situated during the season of Christmas. These movies might dive into the heartwarming chaos of festive traditions, the spirit of goodwill, family shenanigans, and of course, the occasional leap off skyscrapers with a hose tied around your waist. Because nothing says 'Merry Christmas' like a bit of high-stakes action mixed with holiday magic! Or it might just have a bloke in a Santa suit.
